Same shell, two answers for the controls

Both take the language from your chart demo: the plum hairline and footer, the slim white title bar with an italic qualifier and a muted nav, the light page, the soft-shadowed card. Both delete the analyzer readout and recolour the moment lane. They disagree on exactly one thing — where the controls live.

A1 — Light shell, dark controls

The page is yours; the player keeps a conventional dark control bar, because it sits against video. Every video player on the web does this, and for a low-vision viewer the white-on-near-black bar is the highest-contrast surface in the app.
